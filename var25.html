me

from my devils

to the sky

like a stone over the surface of this duck pond

like a switchblade

as a slingshot

up like dust in a shaft of light

in your downpour.

on your compass

through the cities of the dead.

to your sutures

through the hourglass.

into flour

from your songbook.

time in columns

to the ribs of your dome, and

my back.
